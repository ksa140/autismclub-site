About Us

About AutismClub


AutismClub is a specialized educational institution located in Surrey, Greater Vancouver, dedicated to supporting autistic children and young adults. Our goal is to help them connect with a broader world and unlock their full potential.

We strive to provide structured educational programs and diverse experiences, enabling autistic individuals to overcome social isolation, build confidence, and achieve greater independence in their daily lives.
