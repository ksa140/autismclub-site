Events

BIRTHDAY PARTY

A monthly celebration where children with autism enjoy cake, games, and community. A joyful and inclusive environment for all.

CHRISTMAS CELEBRATION

A festive gathering with music, decorations, gift exchange, and activities that bring the holiday spirit to our Autism Club community.

ROBOTICS COMPETITION

An exciting annual event where participants team up to design, build, and showcase their robots in a friendly and fun-filled competition.

LEGO LEAGUE COMPETITION

Registered participants build LEGO-based robots to solve real-world challenges in an official FLL tournament. Encourages STEM skills, teamwork, and innovation.
