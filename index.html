LEGO CLUB

We offer structured LEGO building sessions for children and young adults (ages 5-45), running in three-month terms with increasing levels of difficulty. These sessions support fine motor skills, spatial reasoning, cognitive growth, and communication. Research shows they also help reduce repetitive behaviors and build confidence, independence, and engagement.

MEDIA CREATOR CLUB

The Media Creators Club is a six-month career program for participants aged 18 and above who want to pursue a future in digital media and content creation. It covers AI-powered visual design and video editing using tools like Midjourney, ChatGPT, CapCut, and OpenAI Sora, along with platform-specific storytelling and content strategies for YouTube, Instagram, and more. Participants also learn online marketing, monetization, and how to build a personal media brand or freelance career. The program empowers creative individuals to grow their digital influence and step confidently into a future shaped by their unique voice and vision.

About Us

About AutismClub


AutismClub is a specialized educational institution located in Surrey, Greater Vancouver, dedicated to supporting autistic children and young adults. Our goal is to help them connect with a broader world and unlock their full potential.

We strive to provide structured educational programs and diverse experiences, enabling autistic individuals to overcome social isolation, build confidence, and achieve greater independence in their daily lives.


Customized Educational Programs and Goals

AutismClub offers specialized curricula tailored to the diverse interests and needs of autistic children and young adults.


Development of Creative Thinking and Problem-Solving Skills:

• Creative LEGO building programs to enhance logical thinking and creativity

• Robotics and coding education to develop programming fundamentals and technical problem-solving skills


Digital Media and AI Education:

• Media education (photography, video shooting, and editing) to improve visual expression skills

• AI tools training for image generation, video editing, and AI-based content creation


Physical and Social Skills Development:

• Sports and physical activities to promote physical development and teamwork

• Social skills training and cooperative activities to strengthen communication abilities


Independence and Career Preparation:

• For those under 18: After-school programs that provide continuous learning opportunities

• For those 18 and older: AI-based training in writing, image creation, video content production, and digital marketing, enabling them to manage their own YouTube, Facebook, and other social media channels

• Career training programs aimed at employment and self-sufficiency


Future Vision and Social Mission

AutismClub aspires to grow beyond an educational institution into a social enterprise that helps autistic children and young adults discover their unique abilities and develop them into meaningful, independent lives.

We are committed to creating practical educational programs that allow our students to leverage their strengths, create new career opportunities, and play active roles in today’s digital world.

Our mission is to support autistic individuals in leading self-sufficient, fulfilling lives and to ensure they can actively participate as valuable members of society.

Through continuous research and innovation, we aim to provide an inclusive and progressive educational environment where every child can maximize their potential and thrive in a broader world. AutismClub remains dedicated to empowering all students to achieve their dreams and build a brighter future.
