Music Club

Guitar Club

Learn rhythm, chords, and picking techniques while building confidence through music.

Drum Club

Explore beat, tempo, and coordination through structured drumming activities

Ukulele Club

Play fun songs and develop fine motor skills using this small, approachable instrument.

Music Composition Club

Create your own music using digital tools and express creativity through sound.



The Music Club invites children and young adults (ages 5–45) to explore instruments like guitar, drums, ukulele, and digital music creation using DAWs such as GarageBand and FL Studio. Structured in three-month terms, the program supports auditory processing, rhythm, and emotional expression while building confidence, creativity, and self-expression.
