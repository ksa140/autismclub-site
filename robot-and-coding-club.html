Robot and Coding Club

Empower Learning through Robotics & Coding

Our Robot and Coding Club introduces children (ages 5 to 18) and young adults to the fundamentals of programming and hands-on engineering in an engaging, supportive environment.

Participants are grouped by similar age to ensure a comfortable pace and learning experience.

Programs follow a three-month cycle, with each term building on the last to develop stronger coding logic and technical problem-solving skills.
