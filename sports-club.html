Sports Club

Golf Club

Practice focus, posture, and coordination through structured golf activities

Running Club

Build endurance, pacing, and discipline through guided running sessions


The Sports Club provides a supportive space for children (ages 5–18) and young adults to develop physical skills through focused programs in golf and running. Each three-month term introduces new techniques to build coordination, confidence, and self-discipline. These activities support motor planning, emotional regulation, and social connection while promoting a positive, active lifestyle.
